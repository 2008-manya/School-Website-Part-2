Junior National Taekwondo Championship 2019

Samaira Mohapatra had participated in 34th ITF Taekwondo National Junior Championships held at Goa on 28 Dec 2019. She won a Bronze Medal. She is a black stripe holder.

Khelo India Youth Games 2020 (Assam) – Lawn Bowl

The Khelo India Youth Games 2020 were held at Guwahati (Assam) from 10th to 22nd January 2020. Sixteen state teams participated under the sport Lawn Bowls. Six of our school students represented Delhi and won Silver medal under two event categories. Girls Tanvi Chopra IX-B Preksha Khate X-O Ayana Keshwani VII-J Boys Vikram Rajak VIII-I Krishnav Sharma XI-V Vivaan Goyal IX-F Delhi secured the overall Third position in the Khelo India Youth Games 2020 Lawn Bowl Event.

Zone Inter DPS Basketball U-14 Girls Championship 2019

Zone U14 Girls Basketball inter DPS Championship was held at DPS International Saket from 10th -12th Oct 2019. DPS RK Puram Won the 1st Runner Up Trophy and Qualified for the Nationals to be held at DPS Vasant Kunj.
